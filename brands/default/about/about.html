Journal Archives

Journal Archives is a Jisc-funded service available via subscription to UK HE and FE institutions and Research Councils who are full Jisc Collections members. Subscription options vary for HE and FE institutions and a free 30 day trial is available to eligible institutions.

For any enquiries about subscription options please contact the Jisc Collections helpdesk at: help.digitalresources@jisc.ac.uk.

UK Higher Education (HE) and Research Councils

There are two subscription packages available that include Journal Archives:

Jisc eCollections for Higher Education Institutions 2014-2017 (includes Historical Texts, Journal Archives and MediaHub)

Jisc eCollections: Historical Texts and Journal Archives for HE 2014-2017 (includes Historical Texts and Journal Archives but not MediaHub)

It is not possible to subscribe to Journal Archives on its own.

UK Further Education (FE)

The following subscription package is available:

Jisc eCollections: Journal Archives and Historical Texts for FE 2014-2017

This provides access to both Historical Texts and Journal Archives. It is not possible to subscribe to Historical Texts on its own.

Free 30 day trial

UK HE and FE institutions and Research Councils who are full members of Jisc Collections and do not currently subscribe to Journal Archives can request a free 30 day trial.

A librarian at the institution can do this by contacting the Jisc Collections helpdesk at: help.digitalresources@jisc.ac.uk or logging in to their account on the Jisc Collections website.

Partners

Journal Archives has been developed on behalf of Jisc in partnership with Knowledge Integration, Sero Consulting and Gooii.

Knowledge Integration specialises in the development of high quality open source products and components based around open standards and specifications.
