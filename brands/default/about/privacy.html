Privacy

This privacy notice was last updated on 24 May 2018.

An overview of how we process personal data for Journal Archives.

The information on this page supplements Jisc's standard privacy notice.

Example 1: An individual gives feedback on the Journal Archives service using the survey tool and we are processing personal data - https://jisc.researchfeedback.net/wh/s.asp?k=149424860464&u=journalarchives

By providing information to the Journal Archives feedback survey you agree that you have asked us to process it as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice). You may instruct us to stop processing it at any time by e-mailing journalarchives@jisc.ac.uk or help@jisc.ac.uk with your email address: this will remove your data from future statistics and reports.

Example 2: An individual participates in the Journal Archives User Satisfaction Survey and we are processing personal data

By providing information to the Journal Archives User Satisfaction Survey you agree that you have asked us to process it as described in our standard privacy notice (at https://www.jisc.ac.uk/website/privacy-notice). You may instruct us to stop processing it at any time by e-mailing journalarchives@jisc.ac.uk or help@jisc.ac.uk with your email address: this will remove your data from future statistics and reports.
