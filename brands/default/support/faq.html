Frequently asked questions

Can i access Journal Archives?

Journal Archives is available via subscription to UK Higher Education and Further Education institutions and Research Councils who are full members of Jisc Collections. Eligible institutions can request a free 30 day trial of the resource. For any subscription enquiries please contact the Jisc Collections helpdesk at: help.digitalresources@jisc.ac.uk.

Do you provide WAYFless URLs?

Yes, we provide WAYFless URLs for Journal Archives.

You can use the WAYFless URL generator (WUGEN) tool to generate one for your institition.

  • Go to the WAYFless URL generator (WUGEN) tool
  • Select your institution as the "Identity Provider"
  • Select "Journal Archives" as the Service Provider
  • Accept the default target URL of "https://journalarchives.jisc.ac.uk/"

A WAYFless URL will be displayed for your institution for you to copy or email to yourself.

What format are the articles in?

All articles are available in PDF format. You can download Adobe Reader free from the Adobe website if you do not have a PDF reader on your machine.

Can i download the articles?

Yes, all articles can be downloaded as PDFs. You can download Adobe Reader free from the Adobe website if you do not have a PDF reader on your machine.
