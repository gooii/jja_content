Features

These features are designed to highlight some of the content available in Journal Archives. Direct links to texts and searches are included. More features will be added in the future and we welcome suggestions for new features, as well as content to add to existing ones. So if you or your colleagues have ideas for features, or would like to write a feature yourself, please do contact our helpdesk at journalarchives@jisc.ac.uk to let us know.
